About Us

It appears there might be a slight confusion in the company name. The well-known company associated with water ionizers and Kangen Water is "Enagic," not "Kengen." Enagic is a Japanese company that specializes in the manufacturing and distribution of water ionization systems, with its flagship product being the Kangen Water machine.

As of my last knowledge update in January 2022, Enagic operates globally and has a presence in various countries. The company often employs a direct selling or multi-level marketing (MLM) business model, where individuals can become distributors and earn commissions by selling Enagic products.

The Kangen Water machines produced by Enagic use electrolysis to ionize water, creating alkaline and acidic water streams. The alkaline water is often promoted for its potential health benefits, although it's essential to approach such claims with a critical mindset and consider the available scientific evidence.
